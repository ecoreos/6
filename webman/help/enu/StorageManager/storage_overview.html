Storage Overview

Storage Overview shows the status of installed hard disks, cable connections, and hardware status lights.

Hardware Status Lights

The hardware status lights on the right show the following two colors;

  • Green: Hardware (e.g. power supply, fan, connections between server and expansion units, etc) status is normal.
  • Red: Hardware abnormality has occurred.

Clicking a status light will open a list detailing the status of all hardware, including power supply, fans, and connections.

Display/Hide Disk Number

You may show or hide the numbers of each installed hard disk by toggling the Display/Hide Disk Number button.
