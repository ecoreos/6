Synology Account

At Synology Account, you can easily access and manage the settings of your Synology Account services and eNews associated with your DiskStation and packages.

  • Account: View and edit your Synology Account information.
  • Purchase History: Browse or search the package-purchase activities.
  • eNews: Select one or more categories of eNews to receive from Synology and personalize the subscribed content.
  • Logout: Terminate all the Synology Account services associated with your DiskStation.

To log in to your Synology Account:

  1. Go to Synology Account.
  2. On the Log in to Your Synology Account pop-up, enter the email address and password that you have used for registering a Synology Account.
    • If you do not have a Synology Account, click Need a Synology Account? for registration.
    • If you forget your password, click Forgot your password? for a new password issued via email.
  3. Click Login to access the services of your Synology Account.

To view and edit Synology Account:

  1. Click Account on the left panel to view your account information.
  2. Click the field to change the account information.
  3. To secure the settings, click Apply.

To view Purchase History of your DiskStation:

  1. Click Purchase History on the left panel.
  2. Choose either way to find the package-purchase history of your DiskStation.
    • To do so via keywords, select Search by keyword.
      • Enter the keyword and select the appropriate filter.
    • To do so via the purchase date, select Search by date.
      • Click the calendar icons to specify a time period when you have purchased your DiskStation or packages.
  3. Click Search.
  4. The queried package-purchase information (e.g., the purchase date and price) will be displayed.

To subscribe to Synology eNews:

  1. Click eNews on the left panel.
  2. In eNews Settings, check one or more eNews categories to receive from Synology.
  3. You can fine-tune the eNews settings in the drop-down lists:
    • New product & feature releases: Choose the subscription language.
    • First-hand information on local promotional events: Select a country to know its local events held by Synology.
    • Important updates for DSM: Select one or more DSM versions for the corresponding updates.
  4. To secure the settings, click Apply.

To log out of Synology Account:

  1. Click the Logout button in the lower left corner.
  2. Upon logout, all the Synology Account services (e.g., DDNS and QuickConnect) and packages associated with the login account will not be available to your DiskStation.

Note:

  1. Once you change the email address, a confirmation message will be sent to the new address to verify the change.
  2. Upon logout, the packages will remain installed in your DiskStation, but their licenses will expire.
