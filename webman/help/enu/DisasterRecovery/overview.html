Overview

This page displays the overall status of your snapshot targets and their snapshots.

Summary

This section displays three statuses listed below of snapshot targets (including shared folders and iSCSI LUNs). The total sizes of snapshot targets in respective statuses are presented in this section as well.

  • Scheduled Snapshot: Snapshot taking for the snapshot targets is scheduled.
  • Not Scheduled: Snapshots have been taken for the snapshot targets, but the snapshot taking is not scheduled.
  • Not Protected: No snapshot has been taken for the snapshot targets.

Recent snapshots

This section presents a timeline of categorized snapshots to facilitate the recovery of your data.

To restore/clone your shared folders or iSCSI LUNs from recent snapshots:

  1. Click the calendar icon, and select the date to which you wish to restore the data.
  2. Drag the timeline to move backwards or forwards, and select a specific time point at which snapshots have been taken.
  3. Select a shared folder or an iSCSI LUN you wish to restore/clone.
  4. You will be redirected to Recovery page. For more information on the following steps, please refer to Recovery.
