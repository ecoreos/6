NTP Service

You can have your DiskStation serve as a network time server to synchronize time between different network devices and the DiskStation over network.

To enable the NTP service:

Tick Enable the NTP service and click Apply.

Note: The NTP service is required for Surveillance Station and high-availability cluster. Therefore, if you have Surveillance Station or High Availability Manager installed and run on your DiskStation, the service cannot be disabled while the package is running.
