Simple Mode

The simple mode of Control Panel integrates various basic yet essential settings into a more centralized, straightforward and refined user interface, allowing you to have a quick glance at the system status, set up QuickConnect ID, create shared folders and users. This is the best option for home users who need to manage no more than a dozen or a moderate amount of DSM users, or those who seek to set up or modify few essential settings and get started with DSM quickly without worrying about other advanced configurations.
