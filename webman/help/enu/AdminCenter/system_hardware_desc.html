Hardware & Power

Options such as power recovery, Wake on LAN*, beep control, fan speed and Memory Compression* are readily available at Control Panel > Hardware & Power. You can also set the system to start up or shut down automatically at a specified time, enable hard drive hibernation, or connect a backup power device.
