Login Style

You can customize the appearance of the DSM login screen by choosing from a variety of templates, changing the background image, or adding a logo. To do so, go to Control Panel > Login Style.

To customize the DSM login screen:

The following options appear on the Login Style page:

  • Login page title: Text entered here appears on the login screen.
  • Time information: Choose to show or hide local time information on the login screen.
  • Template: Select a color scheme and default background image. The chosen template takes effect the next time you log into the DiskStation.
  • Background: To change the background image that appears on the login screen, click the existing image or the plus sign (+) if no image is selected. You can choose from default wallpapers, images stored on the DiskStation, or previously used images. Only JPE, JPEG, JPG, BMP, GIF, or PNG image file formats are supported.
  • Logo: To add a logo to the login screen, click the existing logo or the plus sign (+) if no image is selected. You can upload an image smaller than 1 MB, choose from images stored on the DiskStation, or pick a previously used image.
