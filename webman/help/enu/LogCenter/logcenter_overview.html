Overview

The Overview page of Log Center displays real-time log activity, the number of recent logs, the source devices of logs, and a quick preview of last 50 logs.

  • The chart displaying log activity includes options to change the period of time to display. You can click the + or - icons on the chart to change the period of time to the past 6 minutes, past 1 hour, past 6 hours, or past 1 day.
  • You can toggle the log sources (e.g. various DiskStation devices) to display using the drop-down menus located below the chart.
  • The pie chart on the upper-right hand side illustrates the distribution of all logs in the current log database.
